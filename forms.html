WHAT IS E-CELL?

The Entrepreneurship Cell at Veer Surendra Sai University of Technology (VSSUT), Burla is a beacon of innovation and ingenuity, fostering a culture of entrepreneurship and creativity. With a passion for nurturing budding entrepreneurs, it serves as a platform for aspiring minds to transform ideas into successful ventures. Fuelled by mentorship, resources, and collaborative opportunities, the E-Cell empowers students to embark on their entrepreneurial journey, shaping the future of innovation. Within its vibrant ecosystem, dreams take flight, and aspirations find wings to soar, making it a cornerstone of entrepreneurial excellence at VSSUT Burla.
